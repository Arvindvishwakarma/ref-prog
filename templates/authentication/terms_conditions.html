Referral Programme - Terms and Conditions

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Referral Programme of IOTA Academy ("Institute"). By participating in this programme, you agree to abide by the following terms and conditions. This programme allows individuals ("Referrers") to refer prospective students ("Referred Students") to enroll in our IT Training and Placement courses and receive a reward upon successful admission.

2. Eligibility

  • Any individual can register as a Referrer.
  • Referrers must provide accurate and valid details of the Referred Student.
  • Employees of the Institute and their immediate family members are not eligible to participate.
  • The Referred Student should not already be in contact with or have previously inquired about our courses.

3. Referral Process

  • The Referrer must register on our referral portal and submit the details of the Referred Student.
  • The Institute will contact the Referred Student to verify their interest and eligibility.
  • If the Referred Student enrolls and successfully completes the admission process, the Referrer becomes eligible for the reward.
  • A Referred Student can be referred only once. If multiple Referrers refer the same student, the first valid referral will be considered.

4. Reward Structure

The reward will be provided only after the Referred Student has paid the required course fees and their admission is confirmed. The reward amount and mode of payment will be communicated to the Referrer at the time of successful admission confirmation. The Institute reserves the right to modify the reward structure without prior notice.

5. Disqualification & Fraud Prevention

Any attempt to manipulate the referral system, including but not limited to providing false information, multiple referrals under different names, or coercion, will result in disqualification from the programme. The Institute reserves the right to refuse or revoke any referral reward if fraudulent activity is suspected.

6. Payment and Taxation

Referral rewards will be processed within 7 working days after successful admission confirmation. The Referrer is responsible for any taxes applicable on the reward received.

7. Confidentiality & Data Protection

The personal details of the Referred Student will only be used for admission purposes and will be handled as per our privacy policy. The Referrer must not share, misuse, or disclose any personal data of the Referred Student.

8. Modification & Termination
