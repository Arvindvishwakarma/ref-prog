Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the IOTA Academy Referral Programme? +
The Referral Programme allows individuals to refer prospective students to IOTA Academy and earn rewards upon successful admission of the referred student.
2. Who can participate in the referral programme? +
Anyone can participate as a Referrer, except for IOTA Academy employees and their immediate family members.
3. How do I refer a student? +
You need to register on our referral portal and submit the details of the student you are referring. The institute will contact the referred student to verify their interest and eligibility.
4. How is my referral considered valid? +
A referral is considered valid if the referred student is not already in contact with IOTA Academy and successfully completes the admission process.
5. What happens if multiple people refer the same student? +
If multiple Referrers refer the same student, only the first valid referral will be considered.
6. How much reward will I receive? +
The reward amount and mode of payment will be communicated at the time of successful admission confirmation.
7. How can I withdraw my referral rewards? +
You must update your bank details and upload a cancelled cheque via the Withdrawal Request Form. Once verified, the amount will be transferred within 7 working days.
8. Are there any taxes applicable on my reward? +
Yes, the Referrer is responsible for any taxes applicable on the received reward as per government regulations.
9. Can I refer more than one student? +
Yes, you can refer multiple students, and you will be eligible for a reward for each successful admission.
