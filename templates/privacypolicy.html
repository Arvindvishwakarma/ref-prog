Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Referral Program Privacy Policy of **IOTA Academy**. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you participate in our referral program.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal data for the referral program:

  • Referrer’s Name, Contact Information, and Bank Details (for reward payment)
  • Referred Student’s Name, Contact Information, and Course Interest
  • Any other details provided during the referral registration process

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used for:

  • Verifying the eligibility of both the Referrer and the Referred Student
  • Processing referral rewards
  • Communicating updates regarding the referral program
  • Ensuring compliance with legal and security requirements

3. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your personal data. IOTA Academy implements **industry-standard measures** to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or loss of personal information.

4. Sharing of Information

We **do not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. However, we may share data with:

  • Payment processing partners for reward disbursement
  • Legal authorities if required by law
