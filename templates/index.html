Referral Program

How It Works Refer & Earn

Join, Refer & Earn Rewards!

Our referral program is simple! Follow these three easy steps to start referring students and earning rewards.

Get Started

Step 1: Become a Member

Sign up for our referral program by filling out a simple registration form. Once registered, you are officially a part of the program!

Step 2: Add Student Details

Provide the details of the students you want to refer. Ensure their contact information is accurate so we can reach out to them easily.

Step 3: Earn Rewards

Once your referred student successfully enrolls in a course, you will receive your reward as per the referral program guidelines.
